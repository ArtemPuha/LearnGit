About me

My name is Artem, I'm 25, I was born and live in Ukraine. I finished 11 classes of school and then went to college to become a ship's cook. After completing my studies, I did not work in my profession, but went to try my luck in Vietnam. There I got a job in the show room of a local coffee company and at the same time sent my resume to a travel company. To my surprise, I was hired without education as a tour guide and I flew to collect documents for flying to another country, this became Tunisia, after which I managed to live and worked for a year and a half in Vietnam and then in Turkey, the war put everything in its place and now I am at home and undergoing training at one of the best IT schools in Ukraine, Hillel IT school with good teachers.
